BMW Build Advisor — Frequently Asked Questions

Which BMW model should I buy in 2024?

Depends on your needs: 3 Series 330i for the best all-rounder ($43k+). M2 for pure driver's joy. X3 M40i for families wanting performance. i4 eDrive40 for daily EV use. M3 for the performance benchmark. Answer the 5 questions above for a personalised recommendation.

Is the BMW M2 or M3 better?

M2: more focused, lighter, cheaper, manual available, ultimate driver's car. M3: 4 doors, faster with xDrive, more liveable daily. Choose M2 for weekend thrills, M3 if it's your only car and you need practicality.

What is the most reliable BMW to buy?

3 Series (G20) and X3 with the B48 four-cylinder are the most reliable modern BMWs. The ZF 8-speed automatic is extremely robust. Avoid early N54 engines without documented service history due to known HPFP and valve cover issues.

Which BMW has the best resale value?

M cars (M2, M3, M4) hold value best due to limited production and high demand. X5, X3, and 3 Series also retain value well. Individual paint specs and rare colors typically hold better than standard configurations.

What is the best BMW for a family?

BMW X3 (M40i for performance) is the best family BMW — right size, practical boot, great rear space, standard xDrive. The 5 Series is the premium choice for families prioritising luxury and long-distance comfort. The X5 for larger families needing 7 seats.
